About Us

Learn more about our mission, values, and commitment to excellence in education

Our Vision

Vision Statement

To be the leading educational institution that nurtures generations of excellence, combining academic prowess with strong moral character, preparing students to become leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

To provide a holistic education that develops the intellectual, spiritual, emotional, and physical potential of every child through innovative teaching, Islamic values, and a caring environment.

Our Commitments

The pillars of our educational philosophy

Academic Excellence

We maintain high standards of education with internationally recognized curricula, qualified teachers, and continuous assessment to ensure every student achieves their potential.

Moral Upbringing

We integrate Islamic teachings and character development into our curriculum, fostering values such as honesty, respect, responsibility, and compassion.

Child Safety & Care

The safety and well-being of our students is our top priority. We maintain a secure environment with trained staff and strict safety protocols.

Our Priority

Taking Good Care of Children

At Viable Seed Islamic Model Academy, we understand that each child is unique and deserves individual attention. Our approach to childcare focuses on:

  • Personalized Learning: Tailored education plans to suit each child's learning style
  • Emotional Support: Counselling services to support mental well-being
  • Healthy Nutrition: Nutritious meals and snacks for growing bodies
  • Health Awareness: Health monitoring and partnerships with healthcare providers
